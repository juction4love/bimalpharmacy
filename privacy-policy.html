At Bimal Pharmacy, we respect your privacy. This policy explains the information collected by our mobile application and website and our commitment to data security.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following information through the app:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, and phone number for account management.
  • Location: With your permission, we collect approximate location data to facilitate medicine delivery.
  • Media/Files: Prescription photos uploaded by you for ordering purposes.
  • Contact Access: The app allows you to contact debtors via Call, SMS, or WhatsApp. We do not store or share your contact list.

2. Use of Health Data

The prescription photos you upload are used exclusively for medicine verification and order processing.

  • We do not sell or share your health-related information with any third-party advertising agencies or marketing firms.
  • Your data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption.

3. Data Deletion Rights

Users have full control over their data. If you wish to delete your account or any collected data:

  • You can use the 'Delete Account' option within the app settings.
  • Alternatively, email us at admin@bimalpharmacy.com.np. We will permanently remove your data within 7 business days.

4. Security Measures

We use industry-standard encryption to keep your data safe. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is protected via secure HTTPS protocols.

5. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Medical Disclaimer

This app does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. Always consult with a registered medical practitioner before using any medication.
